About the blog

data-guide.github.io is an independent, student-founded blog designed to help aspiring developers, self-taught coders, and career switchers navigate the modern tech landscape.

We break down:

  • Complex tools like Linux, Python, and DevOps into beginner-friendly guides
  • Learning paths with practical course reviews
  • Industry concepts into plain English so you can focus on what matters: learning and building.

Who’s Behind This Site?

Hi, I’m Hardy Fenam, a computer science graduate with a passion for systems, infrastructure, and teaching what I learn. I created this site as a way to share the real-world lessons I’ve picked up on my own journey.

I’ve faced the same questions you’re probably asking:

  • “What’s the best tool/language course for my goals?”
  • “How do I get comfortable with development on Linux?”
  • “Where do I start with cloud and automation?”

This site is my answer.

What You’ll Find Here

We publish content like:

Our Values

  • Respect for Time: Everything is written with learners in mind, no filler, just value.
